Mojo Picon

Description:

Mojo picon is the most famous sauce of the Canary Islands and the accompaniment that cannot be missing in many of the typical dishes of the archipelago's gastronomy. Its exquisite flavor has crossed borders, but do you know how to prepare the mojo picon recipe? Here you have all the details!

Ingredients:

  • 5-6 garlic (or a whole head if we like it very spicy).
  • 2 hot peppers (can be replaced with 2-3 cayennes if we cannot find this seasoning).
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin grains.
  • 1 tablespoon paprika (half sweet and half spicy).
  • 20 ml of vinegar.
  • 150 ml of olive oil.
  • Coarse salt to taste.

Steps:

Today we are going to learn how to make red mojo picon with its traditional recipe, mortar included! How is this sauce prepared?

  1. You have to mash the peeled and chopped garlic in the mortar.
  2. Add cumin grains, pepper and salt until you get a homogeneous paste.
  3. Also add the paprika and gradually integrate the oil and vinegar, until the paste is thick. If it is too thick, you can thin the sauce with a little water.

To serve the mojo picon sauce, you can do it in a bowl or clay plate, with a spoon so that each diner can serve themselves to their liking.
