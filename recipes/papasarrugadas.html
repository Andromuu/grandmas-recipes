Papas Arrugadas

Description:

The appearance of wrinkled potatoes agrees with the name, they are made with the skin that remains wrinkled after being boiled with a lot of salt. Therefore, they have a salty taste, and inside, when you cut them open, they are very white. There is no typical Canarian food restaurant that does not have this dish on its menu, a symbol of the traditional cuisine of the islands.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg small potatoes.
  • 2 handfuls (Â¼ kg) of salt.
  • 1 piece of lemon.
  • Water.

Steps:

  1. First, wash the potatoes very well, making sure there is no dirt left since they are not going to be peeled.
  2. Next, place the potatoes in a cauldron and add the salt. Don't be afraid to go overboard as the potatoes will only take the salt they need, and a piece of lemon.
  3. Cover the potatoes with water and bring to a boil.
  4. When they are cooked, drain the water and place them on the heat for about two minutes, while shaking them to dry them.
  5. Finally, remove them from the heat and leave them covered with a cloth for a few minutes.

You can serve this delicious Canarian dish with the famous sauce of these lands called Mojo Picon.
